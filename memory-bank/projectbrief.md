# Project Brief: Upstream Literacy E-Commerce Platform

**Last Updated:** 2026-03-23

## What We're Building

A modern e-commerce platform for **Upstream Literacy** — an Illinois-based literacy education organization (entity: "Understanding How"). The platform replaces phone/email ordering with self-serve online purchasing, supporting:

- **Individual educators** (K-5 teachers, literacy coaches, parents) — credit card/PayPal checkout
- **School/district buyers** — purchase order (PO) workflow, tax-exempt purchasing, bulk orders

**Domain:** upstreamliteracy.org (shop section)

**Problem solved:** Manual order processing consumes 2–3 hours/day; no online purchasing creates friction and lost sales; zero analytics visibility.

---

## Goals (Measurable)

| Goal | Target |
|------|--------|
| Eliminate manual order bottleneck | < 30 min/day processing time (from 2–3 hrs) |
| Enable self-serve purchasing | 80%+ orders via platform within 3 months |
| Support school procurement | PO checkout flow at launch |
| Analytics foundation | Funnel, cart abandonment, conversion dashboards at launch |

---

## Constraints

- **Budget sensitivity:** Schools/teachers are price-sensitive; transparency essential
- **PO workflows:** Schools require PO/invoicing; no credit card for institutional purchases
- **Tax exemptions:** Most schools/districts are tax-exempt
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA required
- **Trust:** PCI compliance via hosted payment (no card data on servers)
- **Seasonality:** 3–5x traffic spikes (back-to-school, January)

---

## Key Success Metrics (MVP)

- Conversion rate: 2.0%
- Cart abandonment: < 75%
- Checkout success rate: > 95%
- Manual processing time: < 30 min/day
